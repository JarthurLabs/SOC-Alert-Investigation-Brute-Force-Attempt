# How to Discuss This Repository

## Strong Explanation

This work shows a SOC triage process for a suspicious authentication alert. It validates the alert, reviews related logs, builds a timeline, determines disposition, and recommends containment actions.

## Best Talking Points

- The successful login after failed attempts is the key risk indicator.
- The timeline turns raw logs into an investigation story.
- Containment is tied directly to the suspected account risk.
- MFA review is included as a control improvement.
- The conclusion is cautious and evidence-based.

## What Not to Claim

Do not claim this is a real breach, real incident, or production SOC investigation.
